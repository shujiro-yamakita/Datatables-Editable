DataTable-Editable - Example #1

No options with array-based datatable rows

Clicking(Touching) on a cell turns its color and focus on it. Click(Touch) it again or press F2 key to make it editable. Editing is confirmed with the enter key or tab key. You can move your focus with arrow keys, tab key or enter key. "add" button make new line data. "delete" button delete a line which focused cell
